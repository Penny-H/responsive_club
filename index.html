Practice and learn in a friendly environment!

Join our classes, where you can learn from our top tutors! We run courses on a variety of different painting and drawing techniques, as well as sculpture and pottery.

We also run untutored still life sessions where you can choose your own favorite medium and take inspriation from the arrangements we set up for you.

Our regular classes and events are a great opportunity to socialise and meet other artists.

Show your work!

Learn how to enter your work in our exhibitions so you can gain an audience and make sales!
